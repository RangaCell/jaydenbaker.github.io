Fequently Asked Questions

Here are some questions that could be on your mind, if you can't find the answer to your question look at our contacts!

What is "Jayden's Game Recommendations"?

Jayden's Game Recommendations is a website created by Jayden to recommend video games for teenagers and adults.

The games we mostly recommend are multiplayer games as these games are better to play with friends.

If you're looking for a fun single player, or a multiplayer fps shooter, the game should be on our page website!

What do we do?

We recommend video games, what else would we do?

We usually recommend multiplayer games, some of the game we recommend can have a single player mode too.

Do you mention the recommended specs on the games you recommended games?

Yes, we mention the recommended specs on each game we recommend, make sure you read them before buying a game we recommend as people can buy games without being aware that the game can run poorly if the recommended or minimum specifications are not met.

Do you take photos of the games you recommend?

Yes! We share at least 2 images of the games we recommend.

Do you share the links to the games you recommend?

Yes, we include the links to the games we recommend. This is another way of how you can look at the contents of the game or how you can buy it, mainly the links will take you to a steam store page.

Are all the games recommended free?

Only some of the games we recommend are free, there are some games listed that can cost money. We will list the cost on each game we recommend.

Will you show the platforms the game is on?

Yes, we will show if the game is on a newer or older console or phone.

What if the game is on an older platform? What can i do?

Some games may need an older console as the game could be an exclusive or the time when it was released. If you want to play an older game on PC, it is still available you don't need to go back to an old PC, there can be a chance that the game can be bought digitally on a console, however there can be these signs of why you can't download or buy the older game you are looking for, here are some signs below. (For both old consoles and PC)

  • The game may have been privated, taken down or shut down
  • The game may have a remaster from the original version
  • The console or PC store or client may have shut down
  • Some games may be disk only
  • That game may be unavailable in your region
  • The game may not have updated enough for the game to be played
